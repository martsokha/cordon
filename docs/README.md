# Design Documents

## Design Pillars

1. **Every deal is a decision.** No transaction is purely mechanical. Prices, trust, faction standing, and risk are all intertwined.
2. **The Zone is alive.** Events happen whether you're ready or not — surges, faction wars, creature swarms. You react and adapt.
3. **Information is currency.** Knowing what's happening outside your bunker is as valuable as any relic. Buy rumors, intercept documents, piece together what's coming.
4. **Tension over action.** The player never fires a gun. Danger is expressed through who walks in, what they want, and what happens if you refuse.
